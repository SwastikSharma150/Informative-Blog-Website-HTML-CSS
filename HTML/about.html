About The Website:

This website celebrates North American beavers which are very prominent in Canada. Beavers have been a symbol for canadian biodiversity for a long time and this website serves as a source for information regarding beaver's population, dietary habits, living habitat and conservation. All information is verified and all the media such as images, audio and youtube are sourced from royalty Free platforms.
